# Forest Region (first region concept)

Status: [PHASE 0 — Establish]

## Concept
- A **starting town**; the next town lies beyond a forest.
- Inside the forest, a road **forks** — no signage, no directed choice.
  Player may follow either path or leave paths entirely (off-path travel
  is always allowed).
- Each fork leads into the territory of one of two static dungeons:
  the **Wolf Den** and the **Goblin Den**.
- The two factions are **at war** in the forest.

## Established behavior (see systems/static-dungeons.md)
- Wolf Den: ~3 stages, beast supertype / wolf subtype, single-use
  checkpoint mid-stage-2.
- Partial clear via checkpoint → goblins occupy the cleared section;
  return visit shows goblins holding stages ~1-1.5 and fighting wolves
  inside; entrance zone loads goblins per dungeon state.
